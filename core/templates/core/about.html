Who we are.



Vital Tech Lab is a boutique consulting company founded in San Jose, California in 2023. We are about vitality for people who work in technology. We are interested in the well-being of teams and individuals through training and development. We focus on person-to-person interactions within technology spaces.



All about everyone winning through listening and communication

Ginger Greenfield is co-founder of Vital Tech Lab. She graduated magna cum Laude from Cornell University and did graduate work at University of California, Santa Barbara and the University of Oxford. Greenfield is based in Washington, D.C. ginger@vitaltechlab.com

Dawn Goodnight is co-founder of Vital Tech Lab. She graduated with a Master of Science degree in Computer Science from University of Michigan-Flint and with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communication. She is excited about communicating with humor and lightness, friends, and fitness. Goodnight is based in San Jose, California. dawn@vitaltechlab.com
